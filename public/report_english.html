Monthly Strategic Report: Zone Analysis and Rental Pricing

1. Executive Summary

This report analyzes passenger behavior at Chiang Mai Airport during August 1–12, 2018, using the Smart Vision Dashboard. It highlights high-traffic zones, passenger waiting patterns, and retail engagement to inform strategic rental pricing and layout planning.

2. Key Findings

  • Total Passengers: 178,268
  • Average Daily Passengers: ~14,855
  • Peak Hours: 09:00–12:00
  • Average Dwell Time: 22 minutes
  • Most Visited Gate: Gate B2
  • Total Stores: 37
  • Top Performing Store: King Power L2
  • Most Visited Store: Café Amazon
  • Store Entries: 2,540 | Pass-bys: 5,300
  • Average Time in Store: 6.5 minutes

3. Objectives

Short-Term:

  • Identify hot/cold zones for optimized rental pricing
  • Align rental rates with actual zone usage

Long-Term:

  • Redesign layouts based on passenger movement
  • Enable real-time zoning through dashboard analytics

4. Recommendations & Insights

  • Apply tiered pricing: Hot Zones (+10–25%), Neutral (standard), Cold Zones (–10–20%) with incentives
  • Install promotional kiosks and key area signage
  • Improve flow near immigration and baggage claim

6. Cluster Analysis from Sankey Diagram

Passenger movement paths were grouped into major behavioral clusters:

  • Cluster A: Domestic – G2 → Check-in → Gate A1/A2 → Baggage
  • Cluster B: International – G12 → Immigration → Gate B1/B2 → Baggage
  • Cluster C: Retail-Oriented – Entry → Shops L1/L2 → Lounge → Gate
  • Cluster D: Staff – Entry → Staff Area (Z9)

Strategic Implications:
- Immigration (Cluster B) may be a bottleneck; suggest signage & automation
- Cluster C indicates strong retail interest; ideal for premium lease
- Cluster D reflects clear staff flow; zone separation recommended
